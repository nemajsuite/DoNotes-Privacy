Privacy Policy for DoNotes

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

1. Introduction

DoNotes ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the DoNotes mobile application (the "App").

2. Information Collection and Use

DoNotes does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses, IP addresses, device identifiers, or analytics data.

All data created in the App is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Tasks and to-do lists
  • Notes and text content
  • App settings and preferences

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Database: The App uses a local database to store your data on your device.
  • Permissions: DoNotes does not request access to camera, contacts, microphone, location, or other sensitive permissions.
  • Device Security: Data security depends on your device. We recommend using device PIN, password, or biometric protection.
  • No Remote Access: We cannot access, recover, or view your data.

4. Third-Party Services

DoNotes does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cloud services. We do not share or sell user data.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Data can be removed by:

  • Deleting tasks or notes in the App
  • Clearing app storage in device settings
  • Uninstalling the App

Since no cloud backup is provided, deleted data cannot be recovered.

6. Children's Privacy

DoNotes does not collect personal information. Therefore, we do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. The updated date will be shown at the top of this page.
