Privacy Policy for DoNotes

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

1. Introduction

DoNotes ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our approach and confirms how your information is handled when you use the DoNotes mobile application (the “App”).

2. Information Collection and Use

DoNotes does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses. Furthermore, we do not collect technical identifiers such as IP addresses, Device IDs (IMEI/Android ID), or any form of usage analytics/telemetry.

All data created within the App is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • To-do lists and task descriptions
  • Note content and titles
  • App configurations and user preferences

3. Data Storage and Security

Because DoNotes operates primarily on your local hardware:

  • Local Database: We use a local database on your device to ensure high performance and privacy.
  • Permissions: DoNotes does not request access to your camera, contacts, microphone, location, or any other sensitive device sensors.
  • Device Security: The security of your data depends on your device's security. We recommend using biometric locks, PINs, or passcodes on your mobile device to prevent unauthorized physical access.
  • No Remote Access: We have no technical means to access, recover, or view your notes or lists.

4. Third-Party Services

DoNotes does not integrate with any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics providers. Your usage patterns remain completely private to you. We do not sell or share any user data because we never collect it in the first place.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You have absolute control over your data. It persists on your device only as long as the App is installed. Data is permanently removed when you:

  • Manually delete a note or task within the App
  • Clear the App's storage/cache in your device settings
  • Uninstall the App from your device

Note: Since we provide no cloud storage, we cannot recover deleted data. We recommend manually backing up important information.

6. Children's Privacy

DoNotes does not collect any personal information from users. Therefore, we do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect future app updates or legal changes. The "Last Updated" date at the top will indicate the latest version. Your continued use of the App constitutes your consent to these terms.
